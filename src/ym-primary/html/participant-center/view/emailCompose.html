Subject: Help Your School Complete Finn's Mission


Thank you for participating in Kids Heart Challenge (KHC) and for supporting the American Heart Association! Finn's Mission is teaching our students and families to move more, be kind, help others, learn the critical lifesaving skill of Hands-Only CPR, and know the warning signs of a stroke.

Finn was born with a special heart and has been working hard to help raise awareness and support for kids with special hearts just like him. This year, he set a goal to get 1.8 million families to learn Hands-Only CPR, in honor of the 18 procedures he has had.

Congratulations to those of you who already completed Finn's Mission. Who will be the next 18 to help SAVE A LIFE?

Help Finn, today! It's easy:

  1. DOWNLOAD THE FREE KIDS HEART CHALLENGE APP for Apple or Android and COMPLETE FINN'S MISSION to put the forever lifesaving skill of Hands-Only CPR into YOUR family's hands. It takes only 2 minutes!
  2. Check how close you are to completing Finn's Mission by logging into your Headquarters today! Earn Finn's Lifesaver Award when you complete all 8 challenges!
Your support will continue to make a big difference in the fight against heart disease and stroke by assisting families just like Finn's.

Thank you for being a Heart Hero!!!
