Subject: Help Our School Complete Finn's Mission


Complete Finn’s Mission Today!
Thank you for participating in Kids Heart Challenge (KHC) and supporting the American Heart Association! Finn’s Mission is teaching our students and families how to recognize the signs of a stroke and learn the critical lifesaving skill of Hands-Only CPR. Did you know Hands-Only CPR requires no mouth-to-mouth breathing, is easy and quick to learn and since most cardiac arrests happen at home, it could save the life of someone you love?

You can learn Hands-Only CPR when you complete Finn’s Mission. Finn was born with a special heart and has been working hard to help raise awareness and support for kids with special hearts just like him.
